Our Asphalt Paving Work

At JPM and Sons, we specialize in professional asphalt paving services that enhance and protect your outdoor spaces. Our skilled team can install and maintain asphalt driveways, walkways, parking lots, basketball courts, and other surfaces using high-quality materials and industry-best practices. Our comprehensive service includes RCA re-grading and installation, where we heavily compact the recycled concrete aggregate to ensure a stable and durable base. This process guarantees that your asphalt surfaces remain strong and long-lasting.

Complete Services Information

Driveway Installation

Expert installation of smooth and durable asphalt driveways, providing a perfect finish and long-lasting performance.

Commercial Asphalt Installation

Professional installation of asphalt for commercial properties, including parking lots, basketball courts, and other large surfaces. We ensure high-quality workmanship and materials for optimal durability and performance.

Borders (Belgium Block and Paver Borders)

Enhance your asphalt surfaces with professionally installed borders. We offer Belgium block borders and paver borders to add both aesthetic appeal and structural integrity. Our services include precise resetting and re-jointing of pre-existing borders to ensure a flawless look.

Paver Walkways

Complement your asphalt driveways with beautifully installed paver walkways, creating a cohesive and attractive appearance for your property.
